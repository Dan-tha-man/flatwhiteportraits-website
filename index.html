Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking 4–8 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 6–12 months in advance for weddings, especially during peak season (spring and autumn). However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests when our schedule allows, so don't hesitate to reach out even if your date is approaching.
We offer a variety of location options. Most portrait sessions take place outdoors along the coast — beaches, parks, and scenic trails during golden hour. We also love in-home lifestyle sessions, especially for newborns and families who want a relaxed, natural setting. Studio sessions are available upon request for a more polished, editorial look.
Family and senior sessions typically last 60–90 minutes, giving us plenty of time for outfit changes and multiple locations if desired. Newborn sessions are 2–3 hours to allow for feeding and comfort breaks. Wedding coverage ranges from 6–10 hours depending on your package and timeline.
Portrait sessions are delivered within 2–3 weeks in a beautifully curated online gallery. Wedding galleries are delivered within 6–8 weeks. Every image is individually edited for colour, exposure, and tone. You'll receive all high-resolution files with a print release, ready for printing, sharing, and archiving.
We provide a detailed style guide after booking to help you coordinate outfits. In general, we recommend soft, neutral tones and classic silhouettes that photograph beautifully in natural light. Avoid large logos and busy patterns. The goal is to keep the focus on you and your connection, not the clothing.
Every session includes pre-session planning and consultation, the full photo shoot with professional posing guidance, individual editing of all delivered images, and a private online gallery with high-resolution downloads. Print products, albums, and wall art are available as add-ons. We'll walk you through all your options during the booking process.
We monitor the weather closely and will reach out if conditions aren't looking favourable. For outdoor sessions, we're happy to reschedule at no extra cost if rain is expected. Alternatively, overcast skies often create the most beautiful, even lighting — so sometimes what looks like bad weather turns out to be perfect for portraits.
Yes! We love travelling for destination weddings and portrait sessions. Travel fees vary depending on the location and are quoted on a case-by-case basis. We've photographed celebrations up and down the coast and beyond. Reach out with your destination details and we'll put together a custom quote.
